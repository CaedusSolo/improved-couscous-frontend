Hi there! I'm Ying Tong (although I primarily go by Jacen) and welcome to my learning journal.

After several months of learning in the Frontend Developer Career Path, I've made the big jump over to the Bootcamp to get code reviews of my Solo Projects and meet other like-minded peers!

How I Stay Committed

I joined the Scrimba Discord Channel, where I connect and share my progress with fellow students and instructors! It's a great way to practice accountability on my part.

How I Got Started

I started by learning how to write HTML and CSS code on a website called freeCodeCamp There, I learned the absolute basics of front-end web development: from body tags to divs, I got my start there. Prior to that, I'd already had some experience with programming (in Python), so I'd say it wasn't too challenging, learning a new technology. I'm having tons of fun learning on Scrimba, and I'd definitely recommend it to whoever is trying to break into web development.

Some Background About Me

Hello! I am Ying Tong, a 19-year-old college student in Malaysia. I've been coding since I was 16.


I aspire to be a professional software developer after graduating from university. It's not going to be easy, that is for sure, but I will certainly do my best to realize my dream.
